The Senses of Existence is a thoughtful, probing look at one woman struggling with her feelings and whether she can come to a place where she can enjoy life with or without somebody.

Gvantsa Kereselidze is a native of the country of Georgia. She was born in 1986, is the specialist of law. She began writing poetry in the Georgian language at the age of fourteen. She wrote The senses of Existence in English and is currently writing The Next Life, a Georgian language novel that she hopes to translate to English as well. She has translated books for publication from English into Georgian, including Eat PRay Love. She also has worked in aviation field for ten years.
